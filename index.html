OUR LEGACY

Where Fire Meets Gourmet Artistry

Our journey started in 2010 with a singular, passionate vision: to create an unforgettable dining experience where the raw power of wood-fired open flames meets the refined elegance of classical kitchen techniques.

At Flame & Fork, we believe dining is not merely about eating, but about experiencing. Every dish is a carefully crafted narrative of seasonal local ingredients, imported premium cuts, and chef-curated secret spice rubs. From the crackle of wood embers to the artistic plate presentation, we bring culinary theater right to your table.

"Cooking is an art, but cooking with fire is a passion. We let the flame tell the story of the ingredients." — Chef Marcus Vance, Founder & Executive Chef
