Our Legacy

A Story Told
Through Every Dish

Our journey began in 2010 with a singular, passionate vision — to create an unforgettable dining experience where the raw power of wood-fired open flames meets the refined elegance of classical culinary techniques.

At Flame & Fork, we believe dining is not merely about eating — it is about experiencing. Every dish is a carefully crafted narrative of seasonal local ingredients, imported premium cuts, and chef-curated secret spice rubs.

"

Cooking is an art, but cooking with fire is a passion. We let the flame tell the story of the ingredients.

— Chef Marcus Vance, Founder & Executive Chef
